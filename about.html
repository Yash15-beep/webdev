About SkillSwap

Connecting Students Through Knowledge

SkillSwap is a peer-to-peer learning platform designed specifically for students who want to share their knowledge and learn new skills from their peers.

Whether you're a guitar player who wants to learn programming, or a coding expert who's always wanted to try photography, SkillSwap helps you find the perfect learning partner.

How It Works

  • Create your profile and list the skills you can teach
  • Browse other students' profiles to find skills you want to learn
  • Connect and arrange skill exchange sessions
  • Learn together and build lasting friendships

Join our community of learners and discover the joy of peer-to-peer education!
